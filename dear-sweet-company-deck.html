About Us
Who

A boutique artisan bakery bringing the authentic flavors of Dubai directly to Southern California.
Crafted With
Passion & Purpose

Dear Sweet was born from a love of Middle Eastern confectionery and a desire to bring something truly unique to the Southern California food scene. Every cookie is handcrafted using imported kataifi (kunafa) pastry, premium pistachio cream, and authentic Dubai chocolate.

Our cookies are baked fresh to order, individually packaged, and ready for retail display or personal gifting. We serve individual customers, cafés, event planners, and corporate clients throughout Orange County and the LA area.

Every customer who has tried our cookies has come back for more — and each batch sells out within 2 days.
